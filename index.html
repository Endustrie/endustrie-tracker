          Sync between devices

          End-to-end encrypted sync Pushes your encrypted data under a random ID — the server only ever sees ciphertext. On another device (or your phone), use “Restore from sync” with your Sync ID and passphrase.
          Your Sync IDTreat it like a key.

          Sharing

          Share the whole tracker Creates a read-only link anyone can open — no account, no passphrase. The snapshot is encrypted; the key rides in the link itself, so only people you send it to can read it. Finance is never included.
