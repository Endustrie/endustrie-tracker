Endustrie Tracker — Privacy Policy

Effective July 5, 2026 · Applies to the Endustrie Tracker app (web and iOS)

The short version

We do not collect, see, or sell any of your data. Ever. There are no accounts, no analytics, no ads, no trackers, and no third-party SDKs.

Where your data lives

Everything you put into Endustrie Tracker — songs, notes, contacts, finances, artwork, audio files — is stored on your device, encrypted with AES-256 under a key derived from your passphrase. The app works fully offline.

Optional sync and sharing

If you turn on sync or create a share link, the app uploads an end-to-end encrypted copy of your data to a private database. The encryption key never leaves your device (for shares, it travels only inside the link you choose to send). The server stores unreadable ciphertext under a random identifier — it has no way to know your name, email, or anything about the content. Disabling sync or revoking a share deletes the server copy.

What we can't do

Because of this design, we cannot read your data, recover a forgotten passphrase, or hand anything meaningful to a third party — there is nothing readable to hand over.
